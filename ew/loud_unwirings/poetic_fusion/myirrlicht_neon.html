MYIRRLICH neon

neon

By: MyIrrlicht

We grew up
in pools of bright lights
and flickering screens, 
which glowed over us,
casting shadows over our tensing veins.

We turned on, 
tuned in
to the rush of images,
to the imaginary conversations,
of our parents.

We’re heading toward
the light at the end,
which is glowing green,
which reads,
“that’s all, folks.”
