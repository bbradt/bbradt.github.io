MYIRRLICH Beryllium

Beryllium

By: MyIrrlicht

Your eyes, filled with Xs, fire piercing rays
which penetrate my dome-made skin
but my toxic bones are spread too thin
and cannot be easily seen

you breathe in my dust
and it congeals into weighted nodes
which hang from your limbs, swinging,
like poisonous ornaments

why do you try to see through
but never look inside
why do you look at my background
and not the hot, spinning core
which is the parent of my bones
