MYIRRLICH fluorine

fluorine

By: MyIrrlicht

In those early years,
the world tasted like hyperbole,
but still somehow real.

I’d not yet learned to
welcome the haze,
which would later allow me to get through the day
without the blazing rush of discovering fractals
in a stranger’s face.

Reactions were never at risk
of pretending,
and pretending was as real as not;
systems of belief had not fully stricken,
and nostalgia was just an empty word,
overwhelmed by the present.

But all the same,
I was mostly reactions,
all bonds and no single identity
and such things
really shouldn’t last.
